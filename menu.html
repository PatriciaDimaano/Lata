Cakes

Dark Chocolate Lata Cake

5 different textures of rich dark chocolate all in one can. Layers of moist dark chocolate chiffon cake, dark chocolate pudding, dark chocolate ganache, thin chocolate hard layer sheet, and dark cocoa dusting. Our best-seller and definitely addicting.

Strawberry Cream Cheese Lata Cake

Layers of buttery graham crust, madagascar vanilla chiffon cake, strawberry compote, cream cheese mousse, topped with pillowy vanilla cake crumbs. Sweet and tart just like strawberry yogurt!

Raspberry Hoji-Matcha Lata Cake

Layers of hojicha sponge cake, mascarpone cheese with fresh raspberries, and top-grade matcha mousse. All topped with a white chocolate matcha thin hard layer sheet and matcha dusting. Light, fresh, and earthy.

Tiramisu Lata Cake

Our take on the classic Italian dessert. Madagascar vanilla sponge cake soaked in espresso, layered with creamy mascarpone cheese, and dusted with rich cocoa powder. Available in both alcoholic and non-alcoholic versions so everybody can enjoy!

Banana Dulce de Leche Lata Cake

Layers of dulce de leche with fresh bananas, banana chiffon cake, our Lola's secret salted caramel sauce, and cream cheese mousse. Topped with banana cake crumbs with walnuts. Contains nuts.
